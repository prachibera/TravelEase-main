6-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling, visit to Tiger Hill for sunrise.

  • Day 3: Visit the lush tea gardens and Happy Valley Tea Estate.
  • Day 4: Excursion to Mirik Lake and Himalayan Mountaineering Institute.
  • Day 5: Ride the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train).
  • Day 6: Last-minute shopping at Chowrasta and departure.

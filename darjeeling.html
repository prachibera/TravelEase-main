Beauty of Darjeeling

Darjeeling is known for its stunning tea gardens, magnificent views of the Himalayan mountains, and its rich colonial history. Enjoy the fresh mountain air, lush green landscapes, and the iconic toy train rides.

Famous Foods of Darjeeling

Momos

Delicious steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables, served with spicy chutney.

Thukpa

A hearty noodle soup with vegetables and meat, perfect for cold weather.

Darjeeling Tea

World-famous for its unique aroma and taste, Darjeeling tea is a must-try for tea lovers.

Sightseeing Places in Darjeeling

Tiger Hill

Catch a breathtaking sunrise view over the Kanchenjunga and Mount Everest from Tiger Hill.

Batasia Loop

Enjoy panoramic views and a ride on the toy train at this beautiful railway loop.

Peace Pagoda

Visit the Peace Pagoda, a symbol of harmony and a serene spot for meditation.
